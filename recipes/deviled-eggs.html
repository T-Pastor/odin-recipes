Deviled Eggs Recipe

Bang Bang Deviled Eggs

Ingredients:

  • 6 large eggs, hard-cooked
  • 3 tablespoons Japanese mayonnaie (such as Kewpie)
  • 1 tablespoon sweet Thai chili sauce (such as Mae Ploy)
  • 1 teaspoon Sriracha chile sauce, or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon rice vinegar
  • 1 green onion, finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon paprika, for garnish

Steps:

  1. Peel hard-cooked eggs and then slice half lengthwise; remove yolks into a small mixing bowl.
  2. Gently mash yolks with a fork into a paste and then add your kewpie mayonnaise, sweet Thai chili sauce, sriracha, and rice vinegar; mix well to combine.
  3. Spoon or pipe mixture into egg whites.
  4. Garnish eggs with paprika and chopped green onions. Refrigerate until ready to serve.
